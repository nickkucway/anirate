About Ani-Rate

What is this?

This is a simple web application that allows you to create and manage an Anime Watchlist. You can add, edit, and delete anime shows from your watchlist, and also add reviews to the anime shows you've watched.

How do I use it?

You can create an account and log in to start creating a watchlist. You can also view and comment on anime shows you have watched.
